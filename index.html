The Systemic Breakdown

Four critical systemic failures that lead to patient care crisis and data mismanagement.

Fragmented Data

Health records scattered across departments causing clinical blind spots and delays.

Complex Reports

Medical reports are difficult to understand, leading to patient non-adherence and mismanagement.

Manual Follow-ups

Inefficient manual processes result in high rates of missed appointments and poor compliance.

Episodic Care

Care stops at discharge, leaving dangerous monitoring gaps between scheduled visits.

Introducing AuraHealth

The AI-Driven Personalized Healthcare Companion that integrates the entire hospital ecosystem.

Unified Records Platform

A single patient app consolidating **all** medical data (EHR, lab results, prescriptions) from every hospital branch and department.

AI Health Twin

A personalized, evolving AI companion that learns from every interaction and data point to provide tailored health recommendations.

Explainable Insights

Translating complex clinical terms into clear, simple, and actionable patient narratives. **"Know exactly what your body needs."**

Implementation & Scale

Our plan for deployment and diversified monetization.

12-Month Roadmap

  1. 1
    Phase 1 (0-3 Months): Proof of Concept. Pilot with one department, basic features launch.
  2. 2
    Phase 2 (4-6 Months): System Integration. Full integration with pharmacy, labs, and billing systems.
  3. 3
    Phase 3 (7-10 Months): AI Intelligence. Deploy the AI Health Twin and advanced predictive insights.
  4. 4
    Phase 4 (11-12 Months): Ecosystem Expansion. Multi-branch deployment and remote monitoring scalability.

Diversified Revenue Model

  • SaaS Subscription: Primary, recurring annual fees based on hospital's patient volume.
  • Hospital License: Tiered pricing based on hospital size and number of branches.
  • AI+ Premium Features: Value-based pricing for advanced features and custom models.
  • Rx Commission: Commission from integrated pharmacy and lab partnerships (Growth Channel).
  • Geographic Scaling: White-label SaaS for multi-location groups and national expansion.
